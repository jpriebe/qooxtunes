News

What's new in version 1.1?

  • new flat look
  • real-time playback controls
  • improved sorting in song tables
  • "select all" option in song tables
  • added album artist and disc # to tables
  • added filtering capabilities to song tables (by artist, by album)
  • allow dragging tracks from playlist table onto a playlist to add tracks from one playlist to another numerous bug fixes (see CHANGELOG for details)
